# Halosight Sales Process — Stage Definitions and Qualifications

## Source
Structured from the Halosight Sales Process PDF.

---

## Stage 1 — Identifying an Opportunity
**Probability:** ~5–10%

### Objective
Confirm there is a real potential buying motion tied to a business problem Halosight can solve.

### Required Qualifications
- Economic Buyer identified
- Business challenge identified
- Compelling event exists
- Competition identified, including status quo where relevant
- Executive sponsor identified
- High-level timeframe defined

### Exit Criteria
- Clear problem tied to business impact
- Buying motion is real, not just curiosity
- Next meeting scheduled
- Initial close date range established

### Required Actions
- Follow up with champion
- Validate company and contact fit
- Identify need for a Halosight-relevant solution
- Connect problem to business value

### Disqualify if
- No compelling event
- No access to power or sponsor
- Pure AI curiosity with no initiative
- No defined business problem

---

## Stage 2 — Determine Problem / Impact
**Probability:** ~10–25%

### Objective
Deeply define the operational problem and its business impact.

### Required Qualifications
- Refined timeline and budget, or clear budget path
- Decision criteria identified
- Decision process outlined

### Exit Criteria
- Problem clearly defined in operational terms
- Impact understood in terms of time, revenue, risk, inefficiency, or visibility
- Buying process is visible

### Required Actions
- Gather competition intelligence
- Involve internal team

### Disqualify if
- Problem is vague or not tied to measurable impact
- No decision process clarity
- Budget and timeline completely unknown with no path

---

## Stage 3 — Validate Benefits & Value
**Probability:** ~25–50%

### Objective
Prove that solving the problem creates meaningful business value.

### Required Qualifications
- Pricing proposal framework started
- Implementation scope defined
- Business value articulated
- ROI articulated
- Political influence map created

### Exit Criteria
- Clear ROI or value case exists
- Stakeholders aligned on impact
- Internal political landscape understood

### Required Actions
- Build political influence map
- Define implementation scope
- Quantify value

### Disqualify if
- No measurable value
- No internal alignment
- Cannot identify who influences the decision

---

## Stage 4 — Confirm Value with Power
**Probability:** ~50%

### Objective
Validate the value directly with the economic buyer.

### Required Qualifications
- Mutual close plan initiated
- Refined pricing proposal
- Verbal selection signal or equivalent explicit buyer preference

### Exit Criteria
- Economic buyer agrees with the Why Buy story
- Clear understanding of consequences of no decision
- Buyer signals Halosight is the preferred direction

### Required Actions
- Test solution with coach or champion before presenting to power
- Deliver validated value narrative to economic buyer

### Disqualify if
- No access to economic buyer
- Champion cannot influence the decision
- Buyer does not agree with the business case

---

## Stage 5 — Negotiating & Mutual Plan
**Probability:** ~80%

### Objective
Align all stakeholders on how the deal will close and be implemented.

### Required Qualifications
- Internal approvals complete
- Proposal delivered
- Final implementation plan defined
- Pricing agreement reached
- Terms and conditions agreed
- Order form aligned
- Mutual close plan exists

### Exit Criteria
- Mutual close plan agreed
- No major open objections
- Procurement and legal process understood and in motion

### Required Actions
- Maintain tight communication cadence, ideally every 1–2 days
- Inspect progress continuously
- Lock in close plan agreement

### Disqualify if
- No mutual close plan
- Pricing not aligned
- Legal or procurement unknown or blocked

---

## Stage 6 — Finalizing Closure
**Probability:** ~95%

### Objective
Complete all formal approvals and secure signed agreement.

### Required Qualifications
- Final proposal approved by customer
- All approvals secured
- Purchase order process defined
- Signature process confirmed

### Exit Criteria
- Contracts ready for execution
- All stakeholders aligned
- No remaining blockers

### Required Actions
- Drive signature process
- Remove last-minute friction

### Disqualify if
- Approval stalls
- Legal or procurement introduces new blockers

---

## Stage 7 — Pending Closed
**Probability:** ~100%

### Objective
Deal is closed; finalize administrative completion.

### Required Qualifications
- Fully executed agreements

### Exit Criteria
- Customer invoiced

### Required Actions
- Process invoicing

## Cross-Stage Qualification Frames

### Why Buy Anything
- Real problem exists
- Connected to business value
- Solution category identified
- Meeting cadence established
- Close date range defined

### Why Buy Halosight
- Champion identified
- Solution criteria defined
- Decision process understood
- Budget and timeline validated
- Differentiation versus competition clear

### Why Buy Now
- Internal alignment with champion
- Competition understood
- Sequence of events documented

### Why Buy Test
- Economic buyer agrees with full story
- Clear quantified cost of inaction

## Forecast Category Mapping
- **Pipeline:** early stages where business is possible but not yet power-validated
- **Best Case:** value and fit validated, especially with power
- **Commit:** selected and actively moving through contracts or close plan
