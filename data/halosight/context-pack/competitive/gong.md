# Competitive Context — Gong

## What Gong is known for

- conversation intelligence
- call recording
- virtual meeting analysis
- deal insights
- coaching support
- revenue intelligence

## Approved competitive framing

Gong is strong for recorded calls and virtual conversation analysis. Many teams still lack consistent visibility into in-person and field interactions, and they still struggle to get structured, usable data into CRM from those moments.

## Best positioning against Gong

- field-heavy teams
- outside sales teams
- customer site visits
- gap between conversation and CRM action

## Do not say

- “We replace Gong.”
- “We do everything Gong does and more.”
