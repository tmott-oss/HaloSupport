# Competitive Context — Salesforce-Native Process / “We Already Have Salesforce”

## Core framing

Salesforce is the system of record. It is usually not the system that automatically captures the full truth of customer interactions, especially in the field.

Halosight strengthens Salesforce by helping better data get into it faster and more consistently.

## Approved positioning

- Halosight is not anti-Salesforce.
- Halosight makes Salesforce more usable, more complete, and more trusted.
- You can enforce fields and workflows in Salesforce, but that does not solve the capture problem at the moment of customer interaction.

## Do not say

- “Salesforce is broken.”
- “Replace Salesforce.”
- “Salesforce cannot solve this at all.”
