# Competitive Context — Chorus

## High-level positioning

Treat Chorus similarly to Gong.

## Approved competitive framing

Chorus is associated with conversation intelligence and analysis of recorded selling interactions. Halosight is most relevant where the organization still has a gap around field interactions, in-person meetings, and structured conversation-to-CRM activation.

## Best positioning against Chorus

- field interaction gap
- operational follow-through
- structured Salesforce activation

## Do not say

- “We replace Chorus.”
- “We match full Chorus capability across all use cases.”
