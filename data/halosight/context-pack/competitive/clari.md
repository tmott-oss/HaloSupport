# Competitive Context — Clari

## What Clari is known for

- forecast and revenue orchestration
- pipeline inspection
- deal visibility

## Approved competitive framing

Clari helps leaders inspect and manage revenue outcomes. Halosight helps improve the quality of the interaction data feeding those outcomes, especially from field and in-person activity.

## Best positioning against Clari

Halosight is upstream data capture and activation.
Clari is downstream inspection and orchestration.

## Do not say

- “We replace Clari.”
- “We solve all forecast management problems by ourselves.”
