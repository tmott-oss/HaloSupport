# Persona Battlecard — Managers

## What they care about

- coaching
- inspection
- consistency
- follow-up discipline
- rep accountability
- field visibility
- opportunity quality

## Core pain

Managers are forced to coach off stale, thin, or secondhand rep updates.

## Halosight angle

Give managers real signal from field interactions without requiring constant ride-alongs.

## Strong approved message

Halosight helps managers coach on what actually happened, not just what got summarized later.

## Common objections

- “I already do one-on-ones.”
- “This may overwhelm me with data.”
- “I only need key exceptions.”

## Approved answers

- One-on-ones are only as good as the inputs.
- Halosight should surface the moments that matter: commitments, risks, pricing pressure, competitor mentions, and missed next steps.
- The goal is not more noise. It is more inspectable signal.
