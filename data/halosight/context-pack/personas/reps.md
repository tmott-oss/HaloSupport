# Persona Battlecard — Reps

## What they care about

- less admin
- easier follow-up
- not forgetting things
- looking prepared
- protecting deals
- not being buried in CRM tasks

## Core pain

They spend too much time trying to remember, capture, and update what happened after customer meetings.

## Halosight angle

This is the most important adoption wedge. Halosight must help reps first.

## Strong approved message

You focus on the customer conversation. Halosight helps capture the details, next steps, commitments, and follow-up so less falls through the cracks.

## Common objections

- “I don’t want another app.”
- “I already know my accounts.”
- “This sounds like management watching me.”
- “I don’t have time.”
- “I can just type notes later.”

## Approved answers

- If the tool adds work, reps will reject it. The point is to reduce work after meetings.
- Great reps still lose details when moving fast. Halosight helps preserve and operationalize what happened.
- This is about helping reps win, follow through faster, and spend less time on admin.
- Post-meeting note cleanup is exactly the tax this should reduce.
