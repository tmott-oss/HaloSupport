# Persona Battlecard — VP Sales

## What they care about

- rep execution
- field activity quality
- pipeline movement
- opportunity capture
- coaching moments
- faster follow-up
- visibility without micromanagement

## Core pain

They know customer conversations matter but cannot systematically inspect what happened.

## Halosight angle

Give VP Sales direct line-of-sight into what reps are hearing, committing to, and missing.

## Strong approved message

Halosight helps you see what is actually happening in the field, not just what eventually gets typed into CRM.

## Common objections

- “My reps already take notes.”
- “I don’t want surveillance pushback.”
- “My managers should handle this.”
- “We do ride-alongs already.”

## Approved answers

- Manual notes are inconsistent, incomplete, and rarely operationalized at scale.
- Position Halosight as rep assist and execution support, not surveillance. The goal is better follow-up, less admin, cleaner data, and more support.
- Managers still matter. Halosight gives them a better substrate for coaching and inspection.
- Ride-alongs sample a tiny fraction of activity. Halosight creates scalable visibility across the team.

## Buying triggers

- outside sales teams
- poor follow-up discipline
- low CRM compliance
- inconsistent manager coaching
- lost opportunities from missed details
