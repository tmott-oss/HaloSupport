# Stage Guidance — Business Case

## Goal

Connect pain to measurable value.

## Business impact themes

### CRO / VP Sales

- rep time saved
- improved data completeness
- better follow-up speed
- more inspectable commitments and risks
- improved forecast confidence
- stronger manager coaching inputs

### RevOps / Salesforce Admin

- cleaner structured records
- better process adherence
- less manual cleanup
- better workflow triggering from real inputs

## Desired outcome

The buyer sees a credible reason to prioritize change now.
