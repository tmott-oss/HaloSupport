# Stage Guidance — Problem-Defined

## Goal

Sharpen the operational consequences of the current state.

## Persona-specific lines

### CRO

If the data coming out of customer interactions is late, thin, or inconsistent, your forecast, inspection, and coaching quality all suffer.

### RevOps

You are being asked to produce process rigor from unreliable human capture patterns.

### Managers

The issue is not lack of effort. It is lack of scalable signal.

## Desired outcome

The buyer can clearly describe the problem in business and process terms.
