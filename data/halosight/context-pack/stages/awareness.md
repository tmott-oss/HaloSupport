# Stage Guidance — Awareness

## Goal

Create tension around current-state failure.

## Persona-specific lines

### CRO

Most revenue teams still have a blind spot: customer interactions happen, but the usable signal does not consistently make it into the systems leaders rely on.

### VP Sales

Your managers cannot coach what they cannot see, and they cannot see most field interactions in a structured way.

### RevOps

Required fields do not fix incomplete capture. They often just create forced, low-trust entries after the fact.

### Reps

You should not have to choose between being present with the customer and doing the admin work afterward.

## Desired outcome

The buyer acknowledges there is a real visibility or capture problem worth exploring.
