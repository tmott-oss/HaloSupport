# Stage Guidance — Solution Fit

## Goal

Show why Halosight is specifically relevant.

## Fit patterns

### Field-heavy teams

Halosight is especially strong when customer interactions happen in person, across accounts, in motion, and away from the desk.

### Salesforce-centered organizations

Halosight complements Salesforce by strengthening the quality and timeliness of captured interaction data.

## Desired outcome

The buyer believes Halosight’s approach maps credibly to their operating reality.
