# Halosight Product Truth

## Product summary

Based on current known context, Halosight is centered on a mobile-first meeting intelligence and field sales execution capability.

## Core product themes

- AI meeting capture for in-person and field interactions
- note taking and transcription
- summaries
- commitment extraction
- next-step capture
- key takeaway extraction
- customer signal detection
- time-sensitive opportunity capture
- deal risk identification
- competitive intelligence capture
- pricing pressure detection
- AI assistant support before and/or after meetings
- Salesforce insertion / CRM sync
- manager and leadership visibility into field activity

## Value proposition

Halosight does not just record meetings. It helps sales teams operationalize customer interaction data.

### Approved framing

- field meeting intelligence
- conversation-to-CRM execution
- mobile sales capture and activation
- in-person revenue intelligence
- customer interaction intelligence for field teams

## What likely exists today

High-confidence current-state capabilities:

- mobile meeting capture
- transcription / notes
- summaries
- commitments extraction
- key takeaways
- AI assistant support
- Salesforce integration / insertion of structured data
- customer signals surfaced from conversations
- deal risk / pricing pressure / competitive mention detection
- basic post-meeting intelligence
- account prep / pre-meeting context support

## What is likely in active expansion

Medium-confidence roadmap / expansion themes:

- promo-related workflows
- broader workflow automation
- alerts
- deeper knowledge layer for AI assistant
- deeper data integrations
- expanded reporting / analytics
- more robust manager visibility and coaching workflows

## Implementation model

Do not use “pilot” language.

Approved implementation framing:

- Phase 1
- Phase 2
- Phase 3

### Phase 1

Objective: fix the core failure point — no usable data coming out of customer interactions.

Scope:

- mobile solution
- note taking
- summaries
- commitments
- AI assistant
- automatic Salesforce insertion
- change management / rep adoption
- rep-first immediate value

### Phase 2

- add promos and additional workflows
- connect promo-related data
- expand workflows and alerts
- expand assistant knowledge / use cases

### Phase 3

- deeper integrations
- reporting and analytics
- broader management and operational intelligence

## Important selling truth

Phase 1 is not a limited test. Phase 1 is foundational. Without structured interaction data, everything else is theoretical.
