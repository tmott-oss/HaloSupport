## Company overview

Halosight helps sales organizations capture, structure, and activate value from customer interactions that would otherwise be lost.

### Core problem Halosight solves

Customer conversations happen, but important details do not reliably make it into Salesforce or other systems. Reps lose time on manual entry. Managers lack visibility. Commitments, opportunities, risks, pricing pressure, competitive mentions, and next steps get missed. Forecasting, coaching, account planning, and execution suffer because the source data is incomplete or late.

### Core promise

Turn customer conversations into usable, structured, operational CRM data and actionable intelligence.

## Target segments

Strong fit patterns:

- larger organizations
- high-touch sales motions
- field sales teams and outside sales reps
- account management teams with many in-person interactions
- companies where critical customer information is trapped in rep heads, texts, notebooks, or unstructured notes
- Salesforce-centered operating environments
- teams with poor CRM hygiene and weak field visibility

### Strong vertical pattern observed

- aftermarket parts / distribution / industrial field sales
- organizations with outside sales reps visiting customer sites
- teams managing customer commitments, orders, promos, and follow-ups in messy ways

## Current GTM priorities

- win field-sales and outside-sales use cases
- anchor value on Phase 1 foundation
- avoid commodity note-taker positioning
- connect rep value to leadership value
- support Salesforce-centered deployment
- sell business outcomes, not generic AI novelty

## Approved positioning

Halosight helps revenue teams capture what was said, what matters, and what must happen next, then gets that data into systems and workflows that drive execution.

### Good positioning lines

- Capture, extract, activate.
- Turn customer conversations into CRM truth.
- Create usable data from field interactions.
- Give reps value in the moment and give leadership visibility at scale.
- Make in-person selling measurable, coachable, and operational.

### Avoid

- “We are a note taker.”
- “We replace your CRM.”
- “We solve every forecasting problem by ourselves.”
- “We eliminate the need for managers.”
- “We are a full conversation intelligence platform equivalent to Gong in every environment.”

## Campaign themes

- no usable data comes out of customer interactions
- what gets lost between the meeting and the CRM
- field sales is the visibility gap
- capture, extract, activate
- reduce rep admin, increase operational truth
